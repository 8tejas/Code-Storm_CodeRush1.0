About Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a mental health condition that affects how a person thinks, feels, and behaves. It is not a bad thing, and with a few precautions, it can be managed well. Many people live happy, productive lives with the right support and care.

Tips & Tricks

  • Follow your treatment plan regularly.
  • Maintain a healthy sleep routine.
  • Keep stress levels low through meditation or hobbies.
  • Stay socially connected with supportive people.
  • Engage in light physical activity regularly.

Key Points

  • Schizophrenia is manageable.
  • Early intervention improves outcomes.
  • It does not define you.
  • Support from family and professionals helps a lot.
  • Healthy routines improve quality of life.
